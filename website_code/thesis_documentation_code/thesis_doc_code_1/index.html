About

e-Cycle: The Journey Through Electronics is a mobile, educational interactive designed to live in various public spaces such as recycling centers, libraries and relevant events. This traveling interactive consists of 2 parts—a Making Electronics and a Discarding Electronics kiosk.

Objective

Digital devices require an inordinate amount of material and labor to mine and manufacture. From the mining sites to the clean room, there are thousands of hands, chemicals and minerals that make a technological product possible. In addition, the infrastructure for disposing a device consists of complex processes and machinery. Few of our current electronics disposal methods, including recycling, are optimal – our options take for granted the high labor, environment, energy and health costs needed to create them.

Kiosk 2

 Discarding Electronics

Step 1

 Take the component scanned in kiosk 1 to kiosk 2.

Step 2

 Throw the component into one of the 2 options: (1) recycling or (2) trash.

Step 3

 Website updates to reveal a clickable flow chart of what happens when the item is discarded via (1) recycling or (2) trash.

Step 4

 Click finished and enter in email address to receive more information regarding this project.
