About

e-Cycle: The Journey Through Electronics is a mobile, educational interactive designed to live in various public spaces such as recycling centers, libraries and relevant events. This traveling interactive consists of 2 parts - a "Making Electronics" and a "Discarding Electronics" kiosk.

Objective

Digital devices require an inordinate amount of material and labor to mine and manufacture. From the mining sites to the clean room, there are thousands of hands, chemicals and minerals that make a technological product possible. In addition, the infrastructure for disposing a device consists of complex processes and machinery.
