POLYETHYLENE

Polyethylene (PE) is used for the cable jackets, which is the cable’s outermost layer. The cable jacket’s function is to protect the electrical wires inside from damage and to prevent those who touch it from being shocked!

PE is a thermoplastic material, which means it liquifies when burned, allowing it to be reshaped. It also has high electrical resistance so it works well as the jacket for our cables.
