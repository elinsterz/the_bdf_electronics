ABS Plastic

Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS), is one of the most widely used plastics and is a type of thermoplastic. A thermoplastic is a material, usually a plastic, which becomes soft when heated and hard when cooled.

ABS is very resistant to physical impacts and chemical corrosion, allowing this material to withstand heavy use and tough environmental conditions.
